Pahiram a Book at Give a Libro

January 18, 2021

Sis. Rica Marie Paming

                    Our hearts are complicated to examine because we don't know how.

                    However, the Word of the Lord gives emphasis on how our thoughts and words that comes out of our own mouth
                    can be an instrument to check and monitor it. In the text of Matthew 15 :1 - 20, it testifies that the words 
                    we utter, things we say and the thoughts that we are thinking are all by product of the condition of our hearts.

                    "For out of the heart comes evil thoughts."- Matthew 15:19.

                    The first enemy of the heart is guilt.  

                    All of us are sinners, we made mistakes in our lives. Once we knew it, we carry a burden of guilt in a way 
                    that we can't accept it and its like we owe a special debt that we need to pay. It is not easy to realize 
                    guilt in our hearts even forgive ourselves for the mistakes that we made, especially huge mistakes.

                    But the bible is telling us to cancel the guilt by giving emphasis in 1 John 1:9 
                    
                    “If we confess, He will clean us”.
                    
                    Despite how big it is. If you properly confess it to Him not just to him but also to the people whom we 
                    made mistakes. Take note as long as we are guilty,  and we still carry the secrets,  doing it again is inevitable 
                    the reason why proper confession is a must.  So, we will win in the battle agaisnt guilt - the fisrt enemy of the heart.

                    Prayer: Thank you Lord for purifying our hearts, help us to remove the guilt in our hearts, and continue to stand in 
                    the promise that you gave in the first book of John.

                    Open up your heart in Christ.

                   - Sis. Rica Marie Paming.

Sis. Rica Marie Paming
